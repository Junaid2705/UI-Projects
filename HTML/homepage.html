GIRI'S TECH HUB 

 Giri's Tech Hub was established in 2010 by Mr. Adinath Giri, who had a passion for teaching
and wanted to pass on the knowledge he had accumulated over a period of 10+ years to people who wanted to make a career in the IT industry.
We wanted to provide the right candidate in the industry with good technical skills, the desired knowledge and the ability to make a successful career in the software industry.
