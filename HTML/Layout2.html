What is Meaning Of Rekhta ?

The word "Rekhta" comes from the Persian verb "reختن (rextan)" which means "scattered" or "mixed" [Wikipedia, Rekhta Dictionary]. This reflects the nature of the language, which blended elements of Persian with early Hindustani (also known as Hindavi or Dehlavi).
